# Thinking Cap

## Thinking Budget

- Think briefly. Extended reasoning should be 2-3 sentences, not paragraphs.
- Don't enumerate possibilities — pick the most likely and act.
- No analysis paralysis: if two approaches seem equal, pick the first one.
- Don't re-derive context you already have. Trust your earlier reads.
- Limit planning to 3 bullets max before acting.
- One exploration step, then commit to an approach.

## Output Brevity

- Maximum 1 sentence of text between tool calls.
- No summaries. No recaps. No confirmations.
